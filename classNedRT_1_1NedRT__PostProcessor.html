Detailed Description

Class to postprocess a solution of a \(H(\mathrm{curl})\)- \(H(\mathrm{div})\) problem.

This class computes quantities that are not computed by the solver because they are either interesting or necessary for a comparison with other solvers wich may use different geometric proxies.
